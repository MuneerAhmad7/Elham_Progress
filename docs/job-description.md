## ROLE OVERVIEW

Elham Progress is seeking a highly capable Digital Security & Growth Manager to own the complete digital operations of the company. This is a senior, multidisciplinary role combining cybersecurity, digital marketing, revenue generation, and operational management — consolidating responsibilities typically split across 3–4 positions in larger organizations.

The successful candidate will secure our digital infrastructure, grow our online presence and organic lead generation, and implement systems to manage vendor and client relationships efficiently.

---

## KEY RESPONSIBILITIES

### Cybersecurity & Infrastructure Security
- Deploy and manage Web Application Firewall (WAF), SSL/TLS hardening, and HTTP security headers
- Conduct bi-annual penetration testing (passive and active reconnaissance, vulnerability assessment, reporting)
- Design and maintain logging infrastructure (SIEM, ELK Stack, Cloudflare log management)
- Implement and enforce admin panel security, 2FA, and IP restriction policies
- Monitor security alerts and respond to incidents within defined SLAs
- Maintain compliance with Saudi PDPL and applicable data protection regulations

### SEO & Digital Marketing
- Manage Google Search Console, Google Analytics 4, and Google Business Profile
- Design and execute bilingual (Arabic/English) SEO strategy targeting Saudi construction search terms
- Create and optimize dedicated service landing pages for each business unit
- Develop and maintain a content calendar; coordinate blog content production
- Build and grow organic search visibility for primary service keywords in Riyadh/KSA market

### Revenue Generation & Conversion
- Implement and optimize lead capture mechanisms (WhatsApp Business, contact forms, CTAs)
- Set up and manage email marketing (Mailchimp) and client communication workflows
- Develop project portfolio and client testimonials page
- Track and report on lead volume, conversion rates, and digital ROI monthly

### Vendor Management
- Maintain vendor register with contracts, SLAs, costs, and review schedules
- Conduct quarterly vendor performance reviews; enforce NDA and access control policies
- Manage relationship with NOSCO IT TEAM (web developer) and all digital service providers
- Evaluate and onboard new vendors using a structured evaluation matrix

### Client Management & CRM
- Own and administer HubSpot CRM; maintain lead and deal pipeline hygiene
- Define and enforce client communication standards and response SLAs
- Create standardized communication templates for all client-facing touchpoints
- Implement post-project follow-up process for retention and referral generation

---

## QUALIFICATIONS

### Required
- 3+ years of experience in cybersecurity, digital marketing, or IT operations
- Practical knowledge of WAF configuration, SSL/TLS, and HTTP security headers
- Hands-on experience with Google Search Console, GA4, and SEO tools (SEMrush/Ahrefs)
- Strong understanding of Saudi Arabian digital market and local SEO
- Proficiency in English; Arabic is highly preferred (bilingual preferred)
- Experience with CRM platforms (HubSpot or similar)

### Preferred
- CEH (Certified Ethical Hacker) or OSCP certification
- Google Analytics or Google Ads certification
- Experience with ELK Stack or similar SIEM tools
- Knowledge of Saudi PDPL data protection requirements
- Prior experience in construction or B2B industry
